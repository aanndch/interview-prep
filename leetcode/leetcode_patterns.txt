SUGGESTED STUDY ORDER (7-8 weeks)
================================================

Week 1: Two Pointers + Sliding Window             (8-10 problems)
Week 2: Hash Map + Binary Search                  (8-10 problems)
Week 3: Stack + Linked List                       (8-10 problems)
Week 4: Trees (DFS/BFS)                           (8-10 problems)
Week 5: Backtracking + Greedy                     (8-10 problems)
Week 6: DP (1D) + Prefix Sum                      (8-10 problems)
Week 7: Graphs + Heaps                            (8-10 problems)
Week 8: Tries, LLD (Design) & Bit Manipulation    (8-10 problems)

Total: ~85 problems covers 90%+ of Tier 2 & 3 technical questions.
